About this site

Built for real classroom use

These tools were designed to support doctoral students and advanced learners as they work through the hardest parts of research design: clarifying the study, organizing the literature, and making sharper scholarly decisions.

Practical: made for actual class sessions, not just demonstrations.
Interactive: students think, sort, revise, and synthesize in real time.
Portable: tools can be shared publicly and used beyond a single LMS.
Teaching use cases
